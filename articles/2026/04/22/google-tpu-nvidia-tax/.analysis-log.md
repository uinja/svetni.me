## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** Google's 8th generation TPUs (8t and 8i) provide a massive cost and performance advantage by allowing Google to bypass the "NVIDIA tax"—the high profit margins charged by NVIDIA for its GPUs.
- **Key Technical Facts:**
    - **TPU 8t (Training):** 2.8x performance jump, scales to 1M+ chips via "Virgo" networking.
    - **TPU 8i (Inference):** 9.8x performance jump, optimized for "agentic" low-latency tasks via "Boardfly" topology.
    - **Economic Advantage:** Vertical integration allows Google to control the entire stack, optimizing cost-per-token.
- **Editorial Strategy:** Highlight the competitive divide between companies relying on NVIDIA and Google's self-sufficient infrastructure. Explain the "tax" as a strategic bottleneck for rivals.
