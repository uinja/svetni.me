## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** Google is introducing "Workspace Intelligence" to deeply integrate AI (Gemini) into Workspace products, using user data for context and grounding.
- **Key Facts:**
    - **Workspace Intelligence:** New system for grounding AI tasks in Gmail, Calendar, Drive data.
    - **Admin Controls:** IT admins can toggle data sources (Drive, Gmail, etc.) for the AI.
    - **Gemini in Sheets:** "Fill with Gemini" automates data entry (9x faster claims), prompt-based sheet building, unstructured-to-table conversion.
    - **Gemini in Docs:** Style matching, data retrieval from across Workspace/Web for writing.
    - **Privacy:** Data NOT used for training models or advertising. Respects existing user permissions.
- **Editorial Strategy:** Focus on the concept of AI as a "digital intern" that has access to your files and history to reduce manual context-setting.
