**Text:** (Excerpt) VentureBeat survey of 108 qualified enterprises found that most are stuck at "observation" (Stage 1) while "Stage 3" (isolation) threats have already arrived. ... 97% of enterprise security leaders expect a material AI-agent-driven incident within 12 months. ... ## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** There is a critical disconnect between the deployment of autonomous AI agents and the security infrastructure protecting them. Most enterprises rely on monitoring (Stage 1), but effective defense requires runtime enforcement (Stage 2) and sandboxed isolation (Stage 3).
- **Editorial Strategy:** Highlight the 97% statistic as the primary hook. Use the "Stage 1 to Stage 3" framework to structure the technical advice. Emphasize the "machine-speed" nature of the threats (27-second breakout time).
- **Key Data Points:** 
  - 108 enterprises surveyed.
  - 88% reported AI agent incidents in the last year.
  - 97% expect a major incident within 12 months.
  - 27 seconds: fastest recorded adversary breakout time.
  - OWASP Top 10 for Agentic Applications 2026 referenced.
