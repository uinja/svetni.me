## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** AI software engineering is moving towards automated "factories" that iterate autonomously. The key patterns are parallel model querying and continuous feedback loops.
- **Key Concepts:**
    - **Dark Factories:** Automated software production lines.
    - **Slot Machine Development:** Querying multiple LLMs and selecting the best output.
    - **Trycycle Pattern:** Repeated attempts/refinement until success, "burning tokens" to solve problems.
    - **Gainy Feedback Loops:** Modern models now improve rather than degrade when fed their own outputs.
- **Editorial Strategy:** Focus on the shift from "coding assistants" to "software production lines". Use the Mad Max/Factorio metaphors provided by the author.
