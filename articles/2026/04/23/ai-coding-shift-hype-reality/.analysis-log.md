## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** While AI coding tools are ubiquitous, a massive trust gap remains. The industry is shifting from pure generation to verification and human-in-the-loop accountability.
- **Key Concepts:**
    - **Trust Gap:** 96% lack full confidence in AI code.
    - **Greenfield Dominance:** AI excels at starting from scratch but struggles with "tribal knowledge" in legacy systems.
    - **Skill Evolution:** Moving from "how to write code" to "how to orchestrate agents."
    - **Deterministic Verification:** Using static analysis (like SonarQube) to provide a "ground truth" for AI outputs.
- **Editorial Strategy:** Focus on the paradox of high usage vs. low trust. Highlight the specific challenges for junior vs. senior developers and the necessity of verification layers.
