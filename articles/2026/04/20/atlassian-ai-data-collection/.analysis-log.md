# Analysis Log - Atlassian AI Data Collection

## Core Message & Strategy
Atlassian is shifting its data policy to enable default collection of customer metadata and in-app content to train its AI models (Rovo, Rovo Dev). The synthesis focuses on the timeline, the distinction between metadata and in-app data, and the tiered control options for different plans.

## Fact Synthesis
- **Effective Date:** August 17, 2026.
- **Rollout:** Settings appear between April 16 and May 19, 2026.
- **Data Types:** 
    - **Metadata:** Attributes like story points, complexity (Always ON for Free/Standard/Premium, opt-out for Enterprise).
    - **In-app data:** Page titles, issue descriptions (ON by default for Free/Standard, OFF for Premium/Enterprise).
- **Exclusions:** HIPAA, Government Cloud, Isolated Cloud, and customers with managed keys are excluded.
- **Privacy Measures:** De-identification and aggregation are applied before training.
