# Analysis Log - Aletheia: Autonomous Agentic Mathematical Research

## Core Message & Strategy
Google DeepMind's Aletheia, powered by Gemini 3 Deep Think, marks a significant milestone in agentic AI by autonomously solving research-level mathematical problems. The strategy is to highlight the shift from competition-math to research-math, the self-filtering for reliability, and the multi-agent architecture.

## Fact Synthesis
- **System:** Aletheia, powered by Gemini 3 Deep Think.
- **Achievement:** Solved 6/10 problems in the FirstProof challenge (Problems 2, 5, 7, 8, 9, 10).
- **Protocol:** Strict zero-human-intervention.
- **Architecture:** Multi-agent pipeline (Generator, Verifier, Reviser).
- **Reliability:** Prioritizes self-filtering (reporting "No solution found" instead of hallucinating).
- **Evaluation:** Solutions judged as publishable by experts (PhD-level). Disagreement on Problem 8.
- **Transparency:** Raw prompts and outputs published on GitHub/arXiv.
