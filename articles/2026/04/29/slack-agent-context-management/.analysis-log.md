## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- Slack engineering team built a system to manage context for complex AI agent tasks.
- Naive approach of passing the entire message history into the LLM context window causes slow execution, higher costs, and poor answers.
- Solution: Structured memory with three channels: Director’s Journal, Critic’s Review, and Critic’s Timeline.
- The system employs a "coordinator and dispatcher" pattern.
