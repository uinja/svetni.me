## Text from Original Article
> AI orchestration is the connected, end-to-end application of AI tools, agents, and automations across workflows, teams, and systems.
> ...
> Orchestration acts as a central nervous system for your AI, using structured logic and adaptive intelligence to decide which tool should act, when, and how.
> ...
> It differs from MLOps (technical lifecycle of a single model) and individual AI agents (autonomous entities) by focusing on multi-system coordination.

## Fact Synthesis & Notes
- **Core Message:** AI orchestration is the necessary coordination layer for scaling AI beyond single-use tools into integrated business systems.
- **Key Concepts:**
    - **Integration:** Connecting models and data sources (APIs, MCP).
    - **Automation:** Moves from rigid scripts to adaptive, learning systems.
    - **Management:** The governance and security layer.
- **Comparison:**
    - **vs MLOps:** Orchestration is about multi-system workflows; MLOps is about model lifecycle.
    - **vs Workflow Orchestration:** Adds intelligent, context-aware decision-making.
- **Editorial Strategy:** Present orchestration as the "central nervous system" of modern AI strategy. Highlight the importance of moving beyond silos.
