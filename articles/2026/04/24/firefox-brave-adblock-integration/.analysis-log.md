## Text from Original Article (Summary)
> Mozilla quietly included Brave's `adblock-rust` engine in Firefox 149 (released in March 2026). The integration was discovered via a blog post by Shivan Kaul Sahib (VP of Privacy at Brave) and Bugzilla Bug 2013888. It is a "prototype rich content blocking engine" written in Rust. It is currently disabled by default and has no UI. It is compatible with uBlock Origin lists and licensed under MPL-2.0.
